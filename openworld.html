A Middle Age Open World

Ride across the European lands to discover and interact with the broken kingdoms of Europe. Immerse yourself in activities like hunting and drinking games or engage in traditional local competitions.

The Start of the End is an open-world, action-adventure story set in European lands, a dark age land obsessed with power, glamour, and money. You play as Crowy, a local lord going after a one-of-a-kind crown that is the key to ruling European lands. You can customize your character’s armor, skillset, and playstyle, and explore vast lands where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.

Some of The Things You can Find In The Open-World...

Subquests

Subquests that will take you an adventure you can't imagine.

Local People

You can interact and help or harm the local people.

Animals

You can always go for hunting, might be for food or just to have fun.

Gather a team

To be a lord, you need to gather a team. explore the open world and find the right fighters, and get them to your side!

Raid

After having a crew, you can raid other local lords and take their treasures, you can also take his soldiers if the soldiers want to join your crew. raiding other local lords will have cons and pros.

Hidden little missions

there are some hidden little missions that will take just a couple of minutes, to help you to know the world and feel a part of it. you will like them.

Local Events

you can join to local events of each area, that might be a dance competition or a sword fight, or just drinking and singing a song.
